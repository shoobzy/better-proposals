How to Write a Business Proposal in 2020
(Dos and Don'ts)

We looked at all 189,090 signed Proposals from the Better Proposals platform from 1st January 2018 - 31st December 2018 and asked one simple question:

"Does [theory] improve conversion rates?"

What we found revealed insights about sales the world has never seen before. Learn from these insights and transform your sales activity.

01

What devices were your proposals first opened on?

The big story here is that over 34% of proposals are first opened on mobile devices. If you're still sending proposals that aren't responsive or in PDF format then that might be something to fix.

  • PC - 41.4%
  • iPhone - 22.5%
  • Mac - 21.9%
  • Android - 9.9%
  • iPad - 2.9%
  • Other - 1.4%

34% of proposals are opened on mobile so make sure it's web based and responsive
